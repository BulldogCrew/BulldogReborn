ABOUT BULLDOG REBORN

Bulldog Reborn (BURN) is a rebranding of Bulldog (BULL) Coin built using Masternode+POS Forked technology from PIVX (QUARK) in 2019. Bulldog (BULL) at that time was a transparent cryptocurrency managed by the community to be offered to the Public as alternative to invest in the Blockchain-based Technology sector through the creation of BulldogNodes, utilization of Proof of Stake, and active governance.

Due to many factors that made it difficult for this project to develop, Bulldog Reborn (BURN) was born to continue the Vision & Mission of BULLDOG (BULL) Coin. Through smart contracts from Polygon Technology, there is hope for the future of Bulldog Reborn (BURN) can become a Facilitator in Building Blockchain Ecosystems around the world.
